Research Experience

Matching Equilibrium Analysis of Greenhouse Gas Control and Forest Carbon Sink

September 2023 - June 2025

National Social Science Foundation Project, Northeast Forestry University

Working on a national research project focused on greenhouse gas emissions control and forest carbon sink equilibrium analysis. My contributions include:

  • Leading the empirical analysis component using machine learning techniques to estimate peak carbon emissions and conduct elasticity analysis across China
  • Developing Environmental Kuznets Curve (EKC) models for national and seven major regional areas in China from 2000-2020 using R programming language
  • Implementing Tapio decoupling analysis method to decompose and quantify contribution factors in the carbon emission process
  • Providing data-driven insights for policy recommendations on balancing emission control and forest carbon sink development

Academic RAG Assistant for University Students

Present

Research Project, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences

Currently developing a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) assistant designed specifically to support university students in their academic endeavors. The project is in its early stages, with my responsibilities including:

  • Building data pipelines using Python for web scraping and collecting relevant academic resources
  • Implementing data cleaning and preprocessing techniques to ensure high-quality information input
  • Developing effective chunking strategies for optimal text segmentation and retrieval efficiency
  • Designing and implementing context-enhanced retrieval mechanisms to improve response accuracy
  • Constructing and maintaining a comprehensive knowledge base of academic information
  • Architecting a graph-based RAG framework using LlamaIndex to enhance semantic relationships between information nodes
